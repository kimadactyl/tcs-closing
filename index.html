Tai Chi School is closing down

Thank you to everyone who has supported Tai Chi School over the years. We’re deeply grateful for the encouragement, kind words, and enthusiasm you’ve shown us.

Unfortunately, the project has never been financially sustainable as we have never quite had the level of take up that we had hoped for and overall the business has made losses which we don’t think we can ever recover. With urgent technical upgrades now required and both founders having moved on to other projects (we haven’t made new content since before the pandemic), it’s no longer possible for us to continue running the website.

As we wind things down, we’re selling the last of our DVD and box set stock at fire sale prices. If you’d like to support us, picking one up is the best way to do so.

https://taichischool.bigcartel.com/

Customers, fear not: we’re working on archiving the full course on YouTube so it can be available permanently and free for anyone who wants to learn Tai Chi in the future. We hope that for some of you this is an upgrade to our current offer!

https://www.youtube.com/taichischool

If anyone wants to make a donation towards the costs of the YouTube migration, you are obviously more than welcome to as well - donations welcome via PayPal at sales@taichi.school.

Want shipping outside the EU, or to bulk purchase our stock? Feel free to drop us an email.

Thank you again for being part of this journey with us.

- The Tai Chi School Team
